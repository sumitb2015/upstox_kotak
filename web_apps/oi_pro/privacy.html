Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 27, 2026

At OI Pro, accessible from oipro.com, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by OI Pro and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

The personal information that you are asked to provide, and the reasons why you are asked to provide it, will be made clear to you at the point we ask you to provide your personal information.

  • Email address for account creation and secure access.
  • Trading preferences and strategy configurations stored locally or securely in our database.
  • Log data including IP addresses, browser type, and time stamps for security monitoring.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect in various ways, including to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our analytics dashboard.
  • Improve, personalize, and expand our platform.
  • Understand and analyze how you use our platform.
  • Develop new products, features, and functionality.
  • Prevent fraud and ensure institutional-grade security.

3. Data Security

We prioritize the security of your data. We use industry-standard encryption and security protocols to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, remember that no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

4. Cookies and Web Beacons

Like any other website, OI Pro uses 'cookies'. These cookies are used to store information including visitors' preferences, and the pages on the website that the visitor accessed or visited. The information is used to optimize the users' experience by customizing our web page content based on visitors' browser type and/or other information.

5. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party analytics (like Google Analytics) or data providers (like Upstox/Kotak APIs). These third parties have their own privacy policies regarding how they handle data. We recommend reading their privacy policies to understand how your information will be handled by these providers.

6. Contact Us

If you have additional questions or require more information about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at support@oipro.com.
